About Our Founder

Shri. Velmurugan founded SB Heritage Craft House with a vision to preserve traditional woodcraft while creating contemporary designs that resonate with modern aesthetics.

With over 25 years of expertise in woodworking, Shri. Velmurugan has trained a dedicated team of artisans who share his passion for excellence and attention to detail.
